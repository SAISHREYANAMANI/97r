BY TELANGANA TODAY UPDATED ON - 02:49 PM, WED - 1 NOVEMBER 23
Hyderabad: Commemorating the ‘Rashtriya Ekta Diwas’ on the occasion of 148th birth anniversary of Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel AFS Hakimpet, Hyderabad, organised a marathon run in three different categories i.e. 21km, 10km and 5km. The run was flagged off by Chintala Venkat Reddy, a progressive organic farmer from Telangana and more than 1800 participants took part in the run.
